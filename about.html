About Me

My name is Diana Romero, I'm a freelance cartoonist, illustrator, and graphics Designer. I currently attend Northwest Vista Community College where I'm working to get an Ascociates of Applied Science in Digital Media. I've been doing Freelance since 2015, but it's only in the recent years have I decided to take my skills a step further.

My favorite things to draw are cartoons and portraits, but I'm up for any task. I love incorperating things I've learned into my art, and I'm always looking for ways to improve.
